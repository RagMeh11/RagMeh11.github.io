Research Area

  • My research focuses on developing machine learning algorithms for medical image analysis. In particular, I have heavily focused on integrating these models on various aspect of neuroimage analysis. For further details on my research, please see the publications page.
  • I am also interested in various aspects of responsible and trustworthy machine learning models including uncertainty quantification, fairness, robustness, causality and their applications in medical image analysis.
